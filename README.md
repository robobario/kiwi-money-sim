# kiwi-money-sim

A personal finance simulator for New Zealanders. Model your financial future by building a timeline of life events — jobs, KiwiSaver, mortgages, investments, retirement — and see how your cash, net worth, and investments evolve over decades.

**[Try it → robobario.github.io/kiwi-money-sim](https://robobario.github.io/kiwi-money-sim/)**

## What you can model

- Salary with inflation-matched payrises and KiwiSaver contributions
- Mortgages (existing or new purchases) with house price appreciation
- Recurring costs and income, optionally inflation-linked
- Investment portfolios with periodic contributions and drawdown
- Retirement, NZ Superannuation, and KiwiSaver drawdown
